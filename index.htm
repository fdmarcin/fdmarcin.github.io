Marcin Sędłak-Jakubowski

Hi! My name is Marcin. I live in Warsaw, Poland, and this page is a cross between a business card and a portfolio.

I'm a Technical Writer, wchich means I write instructions for a living. I work as a Technical Writer at MicroStrategy, and co-organize PyLight Waw, a beginner-friendly Python meetup.

I use these words describe myself: rationalist, humanist, buddhist, effective altruist, feminist.

In the past, I've used many other words to describe my work, including: English Teacher, Peace Educator, and Youth Worker.

I care about open-source software and the welfare of humans and other animals.

Things I'm good at

  • English – a C2 certificate, BA in Teaching EFL, and experience in technical writing
  • Technical sophistication – it means that I know enough computers to achieve my goals, and if I don't, I know how to find the answers.
  • Linux, GIT, Python, MadCap Flare
  • HTML and bits of CSS
  • Sense of humor and cultural sensitivity
